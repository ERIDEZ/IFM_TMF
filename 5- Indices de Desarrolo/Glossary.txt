
							** GLOSARIO DE MÃ‰TRICAS E INDICES **

> GDP growth (annual %): Annual percentage growth rate of GDP at market prices based on constant local currency. Aggregates are based on constant 2010 U.S. dollars

> Unemployment, total (% of total labor force) (modeled ILO estimate): Unemployment refers to the share of the labor force that is without work but available for 
  and seeking employment.

> Inflation, GDP deflator (annual %): Inflation as measured by the annual growth rate of the GDP implicit deflator shows the rate of price change in the economy as a whole.

> Political Stability and Absence of Violence/Terrorism: Percentile Rank [PV.PER.RNK]: Political Stability and Absence of Violence/Terrorism measures perceptions of 
  the likelihood of political instability and/or politically-motivated violence, including terrorism. Percentile rank indicates the country's rank among all countries 
  covered by the aggregate indicator, with 0 corresponding to lowest rank, and 100 to highest rank	

> Probability of dying among youth ages 20-24 years (per 1,000) [SH.DYN.2024]> 	Probability of dying between age 20-24 years of age expressed per 1,000 youths age 20, 
  if subject to age-specific mortality rates of the specified year.	

> Regulatory Quality: Percentile Rank [RQ.PER.RNK]: captures perceptions of the ability of the government to formulate and implement sound policies and 
  regulations that permit and promote private sector development.	Percentile rank indicates the country's rank among all countries covered by the aggregate 
  indicator, with 0 corresponding to lowest rank, and 100 to highest rank

> Rule of Law: Percentile Rank [RL.PER.RNK]: Rule of Law captures perceptions of the extent to which agents have confidence in and abide by the rules of society, and in 
  particular the quality of contract enforcement, property rights, the police, and the courts, as well as the likelihood of crime and violence. Percentile rank indicates
  the country's rank among all countries covered by the aggregate indicator, with 0 corresponding to lowest rank, and 100 to highest rank.

> Voice and Accountability: Percentile Rank [VA.PER.RNK]: Voice and Accountability captures perceptions of the extent to which a country's citizens are able 
  to participate in selecting their government, as well as freedom of expression, freedom of association, and a free media. Percentile rank indicates the country's 
  rank among all countries covered by the aggregate indicator, with 0 corresponding to lowest rank, and 100 to highest rank.

> Wage and salaried workers, total (% of total employment) (modeled ILO estimate) [SL.EMP.WORK.ZS]: Wage and salaried workers (employees) are those workers 
  who hold the type of jobs defined as "paid employment jobs," where the incumbents hold explicit (written or oral) or implicit employment contracts 
  that give them a basic remuneration that is not directly dependent upon the revenue of the unit for which they work
