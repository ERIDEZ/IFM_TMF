
			*** PRESENCIA/AUSENCIA DE DIVERSOS TIPOS DE CONFLICTOS Y NÚMERO DE MUERTES ASOCIADAS (Our World In Data) ***

|||| Dataset "countries-in-conflict-data" ||||

> Información de cantidad de muertes dependiendo del tipo de conflicto por año y país
> Definición de los tipos de conflictos: 
	*One-sided violence: The use of armed force by a state or non-state armed group against civilians that causes at least 25 civilian deaths during a year.
	*Non-state: A conflict between non-state armed groups, such as rebel groups, criminal organizations, or ethnic groups, that causes at least 25 deaths during a year	
	*Intrastate: A conflict between a state and a non-state armed group inside the state’s territory that causes at least 25 deaths during a year.
	*Interstate: A conflict between states that causes at least 25 deaths during a year. This includes combatant and civilian deaths due to fighting.



|||| Datasets "states-involved-in-interstate-conflicts", "states-involved-in-state-based-conflicts", "states-involved-in-wars" |||||

> Presencia o ausencia de un tipo de conflicto por año y país ('1' indica que el país participó en un conflicto, '0' no participó)
> Definición de los tipos de conflictos:
	*Interstate conflict: Included are states that participated in at least one conflict with another state where force was threatened, displayed, or used that year.
	*State-based conflict: Included are states that were primary participants in at least one interstate, intrastate, or extrasystemic conflict.
	*State involved in wars: Included are states that were major participants in at least one conventional war that year.

** NOTA: "states-involved-in-wars" solo presenta dato hasta 2011, considerar no usarlo **
** NOTA: "states-involved-in-interstate-conflicts" solo presenta dato hasta 2014, considerar no usarlo **
